Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-08-25

This Privacy Policy explains how Stratus (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use our locally run integration that connects to QuickBooks Online (“QBO”).

1. What We Collect

  • Authentication tokens: We store a QuickBooks Online OAuth refresh token (and transient access tokens) on the local machine where you run the software.
  • Operational logs (optional): If you enable logging, log files are stored locally to help troubleshoot errors.
  • No marketing data: We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or usage profiles.

2. How We Use Information

Solely to authenticate with QuickBooks Online and perform the actions you initiate (e.g., reading or creating accounting records).

3. Where Data Is Stored

All tokens and any generated files are stored locally on your machine (or a path you configure). We do not transmit them to our servers because we do not operate any servers for this app.

4. Sharing

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties. Data may be shared only with QuickBooks Online/Intuit to perform the requested operations via their API, under their terms.

5. Security

  • We recommend storing secrets in your OS keychain/credential vault or environment variables with appropriate file permissions.
  • Rotate/refresh tokens as needed and do not commit them to source control.
  • Use unique machine accounts with least-privilege access in QBO.

6. Retention & Deletion

Tokens and local files remain on your machine until you delete them. You can revoke the app’s access from QuickBooks Online and delete the token file to remove access immediately.

7. Children’s Data

Our software is intended for business use and is not directed to children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Effective date.”
